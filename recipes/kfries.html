Korean Style Fries

Description

The only thing that makes crispy, fluffy fries better is when they are covered in delicious seasoning and they dont affect your waistline as much. Thats where this recipe comes in, teaching you how to make the crispiest, most delicious healthy fries known to mankind. Covered head to toe in korean chili powder, they pack a punch! And when paired with the most delicious mayo you've ever tasted, it's hard to say no to these. You won't even believe that they ever stepped foot in an air fryer.Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 large russet potatoes
  • 5 tbs gochugaru (Korean Chili Powder)
  • 2 tbs sesame oil
  • 4 tbs lite mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbs buldak sauce (can sub sriracha but it will taste very different, still good though)

Directions

  1. Wash and peel potatoes
  2. Cut into fry shape
  3. Blanch in boiling water for 10 minutes
  4. Take out of water, pat dry, and let cool for 30 minutes
  5. Season potatoes with gochugaru and sesame oil
  6. Air fry at 400 for 20 minutes, flipping the fries at 10 minutes
  7. Salt to taste
  8. for the sauce, simply mix the mayo with oyster, fish and buldak sauces
  9. Dip fries in sauce and enjoy!
