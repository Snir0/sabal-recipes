Mango Chicken

Description

Savory chicken breast paired with a delicious, complex mango sauce. I created this recipe blind, and I really truly believe that this sauce is one of the best things I will ever make in my life. The savory chicken melts in your mouth while the sauce coats your tongue, introducing sweet, sour, tart, and spicy to complement the chicken. It's pretty good.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Chicken Breast
  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • 2 oz olive oil
  • 1 serrano pepper
  • Juice of 2 Tangerines
  • 2 oz Cranberry juice
  • 2 tbs balsamic vinegar
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 tbs salt
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tbs fish sauce
  • 1 sweet onion, chopped
  • 1 tbs oyster sauce
  • 2 tbs honey
  • 2 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 tbs greek yogurt

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine lime juice, fish sauce, oyster sauce, honey, salt, pepper and chicken breast. Mix and let marinate overnight.
  2. Once chicken is marinated, work on the sauce before you cook chicken. Cut/blend mangos and serrano pepper until finely chopped
  3. In a saucepan, add in cranberry juice,tangerine juice, and balsamic vinegar and let reduce on medium low heat for 10 minutes.
  4. Add in mango/serrano mixture as well as brown sugar and let reduce further for 10 minutes.
  5. Blend mixture with greek yogurt and set aside. Mango sauce done!
  6. Add olive oil to pan and let warm up
  7. Cook chicken over medium heat for 10 minutes a side, or until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees farenheit
  8. Let each chicken breast rest for at least 10 minutes, slice and serve with the sauce on top
